If you are concerned about the security of your online data, we have a solution for you. Based on many years of research at Carnegie Mellon University and Georgia Institute of Technology, we have crafted a few password schemas that can help you to choose and remember more secure, passwords. Our focus has been on usability and security. Our password schemas help you to easily build a unique password for each website you visit and remember it easily. With our methods, a third party will need to access to at least 6 of your unique passwords, before they can correctly guess all of your passwords. This is a significant increase in security compared to using a set of similar passwords for all the websites.
